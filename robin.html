Welcome to Chandigarh University

Chandigarh University (CU) is a private university located on NH-95, Chandigarh-Ludhiana Highway, Mohali, Punjab, India.Chandigarh University offers undergraduate and post-graduate and doctorate courses in various disciplines including Engineering, Management, Computing, Education, Animation and Multimedia, Tourism, Pharma Sciences, Biotechnology, Architecture, Commerce, Legal Studies, Agricultural Science, Media Studies, Liberal Arts and Basic Sciences.It also offers Engineering and Management Programs in collaboration with IBM as well as an MBA in Banking & Financial Engineering in collaboration with the ICICI Direct Centre for Financial Learning. CU has MoU with University of Canberra for Research & Academics
